You need a break - Yes You!

Why? That's easy, When you work continuously on a task, your brain gets tired as it works just as hard to find a solution to your task without taking a break. That is why we propose a small break to refresh your brain in order for you to complete your tasks more efficiently.

The brain can be stimulated to relieve stress and help cope with difficult situations. Working continuously without breaking will lead to your sluggish body and lazy mind deceiving you. Taking a break with us will allow you to focus on a positive attitude and relax.

Featured Game

One Line

As a snake, your mission is to cover every grey square on the board in the shortest amount of time. The quicker you complete the level. the more points you will score. The game moves on to the next level if the snake covers all grey squares. Control is achieved using the arrow keys or the mouse.

Jewel Rush

In this game, your mission is to move the blocks so that three of the same kind are connected in one line. You will score more points if you can tie more than three of the same blocks. The game develops logical thinking skills and stimulates the areas of the brain responsible for administrative tasks. Control is achieved using the mouse.

Neon Math

To complete this game, you must drag the green numbers over the others to hit all the boxes. the level will be completed when all boxes have been hit and the number in the green box reaches zero (0). Control is achieved using the mouse.
